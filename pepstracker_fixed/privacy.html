Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025 · Last updated: June 10, 2026

PepsTracker ("we," "us," or "our") operates pepstracker.com, a research peptide price comparison platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Account information: If you create a PepsTracker account, we collect your email address and display name to provide account features such as saved listings and price alerts.

Usage data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about how visitors use the site — including pages viewed, time on site, and general geographic region. This data contains no personally identifiable information.

Cookies: We use essential cookies for account authentication (powered by Firebase) and analytics cookies via Google Analytics. You can disable analytics cookies in your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain your account and saved price tracker features
  • To send price alert notifications you have opted into
  • To improve the site through anonymized usage analytics
  • To communicate with you about your account if needed

Affiliate Links & Third Parties

PepsTracker contains affiliate links to third-party vendor websites. When you click a Shop link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. We do not share your personal information with vendors. Third-party websites have their own privacy policies which we do not control.

Data Storage & Security

Account data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which employs industry-standard encryption. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at partnerships@pepstracker.com. You may also opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Children's Privacy

PepsTracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions: partnerships@pepstracker.com
