Within 24 hours of your subscription starting. We manually onboard each featured vendor to make sure your prices, discount codes, and badge all look perfect before going live.

Do I need to be listed on PepsTracker first?

Yes — getting listed is free and takes 1–2 business days. Apply here. Once you're listed, you can subscribe to any advertising plan.

What peptides will my brand appear for?

Every peptide in your catalog that we track. If you sell 20 compounds we follow, you'll be featured across all 20 search results pages. We can also add new compounds to our tracker if you carry something we don't yet list.

What's the difference between Featured and Elite?

Featured pins you to #1 in results for all your compounds. Elite adds exclusive placement (no competitor can outrank you), a published vendor spotlight article that ranks on Google, and dedicated account support with monthly reporting.

How is pricing handled?

We invoice monthly via email. Payment is by bank transfer, card, or crypto. No contracts — cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We're flexible on payment terms for larger commitments.

Can I run a limited-time sale and have it reflected?

Yes — we update prices daily. If you're running a flash sale, email us at partnerships@pepstracker.com and we'll prioritize pushing your updated pricing immediately, ensuring you get maximum exposure during your sale window.

Do you offer sponsored content or newsletter placements?

We're developing additional formats including sponsored blog posts, email newsletter features, and social content. Contact us to discuss what's currently available and what's in the pipeline.
