❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best research peptides in 2026?

The most studied research peptides in 2026 are Semaglutide and Tirzepatide (metabolic), BPC-157 and TB-500 (tissue repair), Ipamorelin and Sermorelin (GH), GHK-Cu (anti-aging), NAD+ (cellular energy), and PT-141 (melanocortin). The "best" depends entirely on the research focus.

What peptide has the most research behind it?

BPC-157 has over 150 published animal studies. Semaglutide and Tirzepatide have extensive human clinical trial data. Epithalon was studied for decades by Russian researcher Vladimir Khavinson.
