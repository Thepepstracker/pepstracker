💡 What to Look for in a Peptide Vendor

Cost-per-milligram ($/mg): Always calculate this — divide the discounted price by the number of mg in the vial. A $30 vial of 5mg costs more per unit than a $55 vial of 10mg.

Certificate of Analysis (COA): Look for vendors who publish third-party HPLC or mass spectrometry purity results. This verifies you're getting what's advertised.

Discount codes: Most vendors offer 10–50% off through affiliate codes. PepsTracker applies all available codes automatically in our price comparison.

Catalog breadth: If you research multiple compounds, a vendor with a larger catalog saves on shipping costs per compound.
