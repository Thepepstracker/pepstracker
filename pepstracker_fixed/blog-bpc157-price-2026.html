What is BPC-157?

BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound-157) is a synthetic pentadecapeptide derived from a protein found in gastric juice. It consists of 15 amino acids and has been the subject of extensive research for its potential role in healing and recovery.

Research suggests BPC-157 may support connective tissue repair, tendon and ligament healing, and gut health. It's one of the most well-researched peptides in the space and remains consistently popular among buyers.

What to Look for When Buying BPC-157

1. Third-Party COA Testing

Always buy from vendors with publicly available Certificates of Analysis (COAs). These confirm the peptide's purity and identity through HPLC or mass spectrometry testing. All 17 vendors on PepsTracker publish COAs.

2. Lyophilized Powder

BPC-157 should be sold as a lyophilized (freeze-dried) powder in a sealed vial. This ensures stability and shelf life. Avoid pre-mixed liquid solutions as they degrade faster.

3. Price Per mg — Not Total Price

A cheaper total price doesn't mean better value if the vial contains less peptide. Always compare on a per-mg basis, which is exactly what our tracker does automatically.
