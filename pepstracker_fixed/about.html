About PepsTracker

What We Do

Peptide vendors sell compounds in different vial sizes, making direct price comparison misleading. A $30 vial at 5mg costs more per milligram than a $55 vial at 10mg. PepsTracker normalizes every price to cost-per-milligram so you can compare fairly across all vendors at a glance.

We also aggregate available discount codes from each vendor, so the prices you see already reflect the best available deal.

Key Facts

  • Founded: 2024
  • Coverage: 15 research peptide vendors, 86+ compounds
  • Update frequency: Prices reviewed and updated daily
  • Methodology: Cost-per-milligram ($/mg) after applying available discount codes
  • Data source: Publicly listed retail prices from vendor websites
  • Independence: PepsTracker is an independent comparison platform. Vendor listings may include affiliate links which generate a commission at no cost to the buyer.
  • Purpose: Research use price comparison only. All products are for laboratory research, not human or animal consumption.
