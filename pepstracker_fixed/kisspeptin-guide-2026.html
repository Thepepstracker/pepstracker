What is Kisspeptin?

Kisspeptin (often Kisspeptin-10) is a peptide studied for its role in activating GnRH (gonadotropin-releasing hormone) neurons — effectively an upstream regulator of the reproductive-hormone axis via the Kiss1 receptor.

Research context

Kisspeptin is studied in reproductive endocrinology research. Like all research peptides, it's sold for laboratory use only.
