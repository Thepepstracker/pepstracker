GLP-1

How it works

Mimics the GLP-1 hormone released after eating, signaling satiety to the brain and slowing gastric emptying. Also improves insulin sensitivity and reduces glucagon secretion.

Research uses

Metabolic research
Appetite regulation studies
Glucose homeostasis
Cardiovascular research

Research notes

Active ingredient in Ozempic and Wegovy. Most studied GLP-1 agonist with extensive clinical trial data. Typically dosed weekly due to very long half-life from fatty acid chain modification.

The C18 fatty acid modification allows albumin binding, dramatically extending its half-life compared to native GLP-1 (which lasts only minutes). This makes weekly dosing sufficient for sustained receptor activation.
