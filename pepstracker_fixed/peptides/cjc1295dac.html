CJC-1295 (with DAC)
Also known as: CJC-DAC
Growth

A long-acting GHRH analogue that binds albumin to extend its half-life to ~8 days.

📋 Overview

CJC-1295 with Drug Affinity Complex (DAC) covalently binds serum albumin, extending its half-life to approximately 8 days and allowing once or twice-weekly dosing. It produces sustained elevation of GH and IGF-1 levels, making it convenient for longer research protocols.

🔬 Common Research Uses

Sustained GH elevation
Once-weekly dosing
IGF-1 optimisation
